## üî¨ **Educational Features**

### **Orbital Mechanics**
Watch how planets move:
- **Elliptical orbits**: Not perfect circles (especially Mercury, Mars)
- **Varying speed**: Faster near Sun (Kepler's laws)
- **Orbital inclination**: Some planets tilted relative to ecliptic
- **Perihelion/Aphelion**: Closest/farthest points marked

### **Astronomical Concepts**
- **AU (Astronomical Unit)**: 1 AU = ~150 million km (Earth-Sun distance)
- **Ecliptic Plane**: Earth's orbital plane (yellow grid)
- **Orbital Nodes**: Where orbit crosses ecliptic (‚òä‚òã symbols)
- **Celestial Sphere**: Background stars projected at 400 AU

### **Time Systems**
- **Julian Date**: Continuous day count for astronomy
- **Real-Time**: Synced to your system clock
- **Simulation Time**: Advance at any speed (0.1x to 1000x)
- **Date Range**: Explore any date (accuracy depends on data)

## üêõ **Troubleshooting**

### **Low Frame Rate**
- Close other browser tabs
- Reduce window size
- Zoom out from planets
- Update graphics drivers

### **Objects Not Moving**
- Check if time is **paused** (‚è∏ button)
- Check if in **Live Mode** (planets move slowly in real-time)
- Switch to **Simulation Mode** with higher speed (10x-1000x)

### **Can't Select Planet**
- Click directly on the **orbit line** (dashed colored line)
- Or click on the **planet label** (floating text box)
- Camera will smoothly transition to selected object

### **Time Controls Not Working**
- Check if in **Live Mode** (controls disabled except "Go Live")
- Switch to **Simulation Mode** to enable full controls
- Check keyboard focus (click on visualization area first)
